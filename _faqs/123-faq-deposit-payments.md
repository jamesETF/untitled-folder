---
title: Is the deposit refundable? How do payments work?
categories: [puppiesfaq]
---

A deposit (typically $500–$1,000 depending on the puppy) secures your reservation and is applied toward the total price. Deposits are non-refundable but transferable — if your circumstances change, we'll apply your deposit to a future litter. The remaining balance is due before delivery. We accept bank transfers and Zelle, and offer financing options for qualified buyers. The full payment breakdown, health guarantee terms, and delivery details are documented in a written contract you review before any money changes hands.
