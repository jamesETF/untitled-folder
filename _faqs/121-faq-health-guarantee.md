---
title: What does your 1-year health guarantee cover?
categories: [puppiesfaq]
---

Our 1-year health guarantee covers genetic and congenital health conditions. If your puppy is diagnosed with a hereditary condition within the first year, we work with you on a resolution that puts the puppy's wellbeing first. Because every parent is Embark DNA tested for 250+ conditions before breeding, the risk is dramatically lower than industry average. The guarantee is a written contract you receive before any deposit changes hands. We also provide lifetime breeder support — if something comes up at year 3 or year 7, call us and we will help.
