title: Which French Bulldog colors are most expensive?
categories: [colorfaq]
---
Rare and exotic colors command higher prices because of the additional genetic testing, selective breeding, and smaller gene pools involved. From most to least expensive: Fluffy Frenchies (long-haired, caused by the Lh gene) are the priciest, followed by Lilac, Merle, and Blue.
