---
title: Are French Bulldogs good apartment dogs?
categories: [puppiesfaq]
---

French Bulldogs are one of the best apartment breeds. They're compact (typically 20–28 lbs), lower energy compared to other breeds, and don't need a yard. A 20-minute walk twice a day is usually enough. They're quiet dogs — Frenchies rarely bark excessively, which your neighbors will appreciate. The main thing to watch is temperature: Frenchies are brachycephalic (flat-faced), so air conditioning in summer is a must. Many of our families live in apartments and have very happy Frenchies.
