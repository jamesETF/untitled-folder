---
title: How do you deliver puppies to out-of-state families?
categories: [puppiesfaq, deliveryfaq, faqpage-process]
---

We use a trusted flight nanny service for out-of-state deliveries. Your puppy flies in-cabin with an experienced handler — never in cargo, never alone. The nanny meets you at your local airport and hands off your puppy personally. The flight nanny fee covers the handler's flight, a travel carrier, and all necessary health certificates for travel. We've delivered puppies to families in every major city across the U.S. — [Chicago](/illinois/chicago/), [Boston](/massachusetts/boston/), [Los Angeles](/California/Los_Angeles/), [Atlanta](/georgia/atlanta/), and everywhere in between. [Learn more about our delivery options](/puppy-delivery/).
