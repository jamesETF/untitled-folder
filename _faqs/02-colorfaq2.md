---
title: Are rare color French Bulldogs less healthy?
categories: [colorfaq]
---
Not when bred responsibly. The myth that rare colors like blue, lilac, or merle are inherently unhealthy comes from irresponsible breeders who prioritize color over health testing. When both parents are genetically tested and cleared for conditions like CMR1, DM, HUU, and JHC, rare color Frenchies are just as healthy as standard colors.
