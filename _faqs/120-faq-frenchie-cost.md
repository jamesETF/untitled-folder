---
title: How much does a French Bulldog puppy cost?
categories: [puppiesfaq]
---

French Bulldog prices vary based on color, genetics, and breeder quality. Our puppies typically range from $3,500 to $12,000+. Standard colors like fawn and cream are on the lower end, while rare colors like [lilac](/puppies/upcoming-colors/lilac-french-bulldog), [merle](/puppies/upcoming-colors/merle-french-bulldog), and [fluffy](/puppies/upcoming-colors/fluffy-french-bulldog) French Bulldogs command higher prices due to rarity and additional genetic testing. Every puppy includes Embark DNA testing, AKC registration, microchipping, a 1-year health guarantee, and personal hand-delivery — no hidden fees.
