title: Where are you located? Do you deliver nationwide?
categories: [homefaq, faqpage-about, puppiesfaq, deliveryfaq]

We have two family homes — one in New York City and one in Chicago — and raise our puppies in a home environment at both locations. We personally deliver every puppy nationwide — the same person you FaceTime with is the person who hands you your puppy. No middlemen, no third parties. We also offer local pickup for families in the NYC or Chicago metro areas. We serve families in [Boston](/massachusetts/boston/), [Los Angeles](/California/Los_Angeles/), [Atlanta](/georgia/atlanta/), [Raleigh](/northCarolina/raleigh), and everywhere in between.
