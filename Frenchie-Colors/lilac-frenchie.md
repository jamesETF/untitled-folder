## What Is a Lilac French Bulldog?

<p class="uk-text-lead">The lilac French Bulldog is a light silver, diluted variation of a blue/gray Frenchie. Their eyes are a lighter green color, and their ears, nose and paw pads have a purple hue due to the presence of both the blue (d) and chocolate (b) gene.</p>

Their eyes shine red under video flash, and that is one of the ways to confirm a Frenchie is a lilac outside of actual Embark DNA testing.

We vividly remember seeing a lilac Frenchie for the first time back in 2016 and being in disbelief that the color is actually real. It was a true rarity running across one back in the day. There is something almost magical about the soft silver coat with purple undertones, and photos never do them justice.

Now, almost a decade and many happy lilac French Bulldogs later, we are trained enough to recognize the color from afar — no video flash or testing needed to determine the genetics behind it.

## How Ethical Frenchie Breeds Healthy Lilac Puppies

As a lilac French Bulldog breeder with years of experience, we primarily focus on health and temperament, the coat color being secondary. To successfully produce a lilac Frenchie puppy that is both healthy and lovable, we start with health screening the parents for the common breed-related conditions using companies such as <a href="https://embarkvet.com">Embark</a> and Animal Genetics. To Ethical Frenchie, temperament matters just as much as health. A beautiful dog that is not friendly or well-adjusted isn't a dog we're proud to send home. There is intent behind every pairing — health first, temperament second, color third.

## Lilac French Bulldog Health — What Every Buyer Should Know

Color dilution alopecia (CDA) is a condition sometimes associated with the blue (d) gene. However, this mostly affects the Doberman breed and is not that common in lilac French Bulldogs. French Bulldogs can suffer from seasonal alopecia that is not tied to just one coat color and can affect them across the board.

The French Bulldog breed is prone to skin sensitivities that can in most cases be easy to manage, and Ethical Frenchie educates you on proper management tools so you can keep your lilac Frenchie happy and healthy.
