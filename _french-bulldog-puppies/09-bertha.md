Meet Bertha!

Bertha is the one in the corner quietly clocking everything — and judging accordingly. Don't be fooled by that calm, observant gaze though, because the moment playtime starts **Bertha is first on the floor and last to stop**. She's a thinker, a watcher, and a surprisingly hilarious little personality who keeps everyone around her on their toes. There's a lot going on behind those eyes and getting to know what's in there is half the fun.

What makes Bertha truly special is her **balance**. She's thoughtful enough to read a room, playful enough to light it up, and social enough to make friends with every dog she meets. She **gets along beautifully with other animals**, making her the perfect addition to a home that already has a furry family member — or one that's about to grow. Bertha fits in everywhere because she's already figured everyone out before they even noticed she was paying attention.

Blue and tan, compact, clever, and completely one of a kind — Bertha is 9 weeks old and **ready to find her forever home**. A dog this funny and this smart doesn't come around often. Don't sleep on her.
