Meet Cosmo!

If Atlas is the calm, Cosmo is the **spark**. From the same litter but with a personality all his own, this lilac and tan baby boy is already making his presence known — wiggling, exploring, and approaching everything around him with a **curiosity that is equal parts adorable and impressive** for a puppy just weeks into life. Cosmo is the one you can't take your eyes off.

His **lilac and tan coat is rare and absolutely stunning**, shifting and shimmering in a way that makes him look like he was designed by someone who really wanted to show off. But it's his energy that truly sets him apart. He's the pup who will grow into your **adventure buddy**, your shadow on hikes, your curious companion who has to investigate every sound, every corner, every new experience life throws his way.

Cosmo won't be ready to come home until **mid to late March**, but his deposit spot is open right now. Curious puppies with rare coats and big personalities don't stay available for long — this one is special and he knows it.
